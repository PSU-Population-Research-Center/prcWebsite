## Our Mandate: Optional Major Title

The Population Research Center (PRC) at Portland State University delivers population data, research, and analysis for Oregon and its communities. As Oregon’s official agency for the Federal-State Cooperative Program for Local Population Estimates, PRC produces annual population estimates and projections, manages the Oregon State Data Center, and supports demographic research and training.

### Key Activities: Optional Sub title

- Creating population estimates and projections for Oregon, its cities, counties, and special districts.
- Leading the Oregon State Data Center and collaborating with the U.S. Census Bureau.
- Providing demographic analysis, reports, and contract services for state and local agencies.
- Supporting graduate education in applied demography at PSU.

PRC’s data supports policy decisions in areas such as tax revenue distribution, land use planning, education, public health, and redistricting. The Center also offers training, workshops, and special projects in applied demography, census-taking, geospatial analysis, and related fields.
