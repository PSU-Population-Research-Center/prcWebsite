
PRC Website Revamping
===========

1. Objectives
   1. Inform the user from the first page, what we do, who we are
   2. Provide CTA button to reach out, within reach from any page
   3. Balance elegance with simplicity for future maintenance
2. Menu group
   1. Home, landing page
   2. Programs, core long term programs
   3. Project, Short term and external
   4. People, Faculty, Staff and Affiliates
   5. Currently, News articles
3. Major templates: 3 main ways content is presented
   1. Home page: mostly automatic aggregation of internal and external content. Is auto update when new content is added.
   2. Listing page, same for all menu landing page
   3. Content page, the most granular content
   4. Blocks, for Call to action and other short message showing on all pages
4. Category,
   1. Every content(excluding list pages) can be assign 
5. Content type: Files for download, uploaded or linked
